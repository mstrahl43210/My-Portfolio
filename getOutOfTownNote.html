Marcus Strahl: UI Design for a Client

The following site was created as part of a simulation of a
client-developer relationship, with my professor acting as the client.
I worked on a team of 3. We received a verbal request for a website,
and we asked the client for further clarifications. We worked together to
imagine user personas and plan for corresponding scenarios,
and then we created wireframe blueprints to plan for
the complete website. Our client (our professor) gave us
feedback on the blueprints, and then each student was tasked with
responding to the feedback and developing the website alone.
My version of the website is linked below.
